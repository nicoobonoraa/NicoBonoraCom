Font: Illusion

Created: 2021

Among the one I made, ILLUSION is my favorite font. It is inspired by some already existing font that create letters by passing through shape with lines.

Every letters are made by 2 elements: a slightly rounded square and the lowest possible number of lines of the opposite color.

As you can see, it's more than a bold font. The lines are very lights and a lot of letters have very few differences between them.
