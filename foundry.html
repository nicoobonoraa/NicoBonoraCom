The Process...

The guideline i followed making this font were, in this order: 1. build a normal well made alphabet 2. Create a connection of details between letters and numbers to make it original 3. Apply it and adjust accordingly 4. profit.

AnD the result!

The result is a well balanced typeface that really gives personality to the project where is used. His sharp corners give the paragraph a look futuristic yet old, creating a point of meet between old latin letters and futuristic typefaces.
