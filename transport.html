Font: Transport

Created: 2021

"Transport" is a playful, bold font. There is only a high cap variation. It's made to be used in big titles, data representation, or as list numbers.

As the name says, TRANSPORT was made to match this cool set of means of transport icons. As you can see in the intro image, this was mainly made for a "poster" making.

I was planning to make this, BEAR and SPACE a unique font with different variations due to their similar composition.
