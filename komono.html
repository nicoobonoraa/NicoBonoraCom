Font: Komono

Created: 2020

KOMONO font is a good font for both paragraphs and titles. There are 2 available versions: one more "complex" and an easier readable one.

Komono is a light and elegant display font, it gives a premium look to the project where it's put in.

As mentioned, there's a more complex version of the font. That is made by a light part and a large-stroke part. This version is good for titles and logos, but it's hard to read, so that's why there's another version!
