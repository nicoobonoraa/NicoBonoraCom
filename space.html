Font: Space

Created: 2021

SPACE is my first font. It is made to be used in titles, not in paragraphs, due to his particular structure: it's made cutting the same shape from squares!

First of all, every letters are created with the same height and width, making SPACE a built-in-a-square font. THis leads to a potential use in playfull applications.

There was a big research about what makes a letter recognizable. It was stunning discovering that every letters can be built with the exact same techniques.
